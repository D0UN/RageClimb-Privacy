# Privacy Policy - Rage Climb

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Rage Climb is a game developed by D0UN.  
This privacy policy applies to the Android version of the game.

## Data collection
- We do not collect any personal data directly.
- The game uses ads provided by Google AdMob, which may collect advertising
  identifiers and usage data in accordance with its own privacy policy.
- The game offers in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.

## Data stored locally
- Game progress (scores, fragments, settings) is stored only on your device
  and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Children
- This game is not intended for children under the age of 13.
- We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Advertising
- This game displays ads via Google AdMob.
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Legal compliance
- This game complies with applicable data protection regulations,
  including GDPR.
